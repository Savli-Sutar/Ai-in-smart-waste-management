    Disposal Guide

    Plastic Instructions
    • Rinse all food containers thoroughly.
    • Keep caps screwed on to avoid sorting issues.
    • Never recycle soft plastics (bags/wraps) curbside.
    Paper Instructions
    • Flatten all cardboard boxes.
    • Remove plastic tape and bubble wrap.
    • No greasy pizza boxes (compost these instead).
    Metal Instructions
    • Empty and rinse all soda and soup cans.
    • Keep metal lids attached or folded inside.
    • Aerosol cans must be 100% empty.
    Organic Instructions
    • Remove plastic stickers from fruit peels.
    • Avoid putting meat or bones in home compost bins.
    • Chop large scraps to help them decompose faster.
